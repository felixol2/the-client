ABOUT

OUR MISSION

We strive to create a safe and chill space for people to come and enjoy marine life! This is possible through the rules we have in place and the common interest between customers and staff.

OUR STARTUP

This aquarium started up from the common interest in animals and the urge to help ones in need. We welcome animals with open arms and release them back to the wild when they are all better.
